App Overview

Tempest Period Tracker helps you monitor your menstrual cycle with predictions and insights about your body's natural rhythms.

Your Privacy Matters

All your data is stored locally on your device using browser storage. No one else can access your personal information - not even us.

Key Features

  • Track your period cycles with a visual calendar
  • View predictions for your upcoming cycles
  • Learn about your different menstrual phases
  • Review your period history and patterns
  • Install as an app on your device for easy access
  • Complete privacy with local data storage
